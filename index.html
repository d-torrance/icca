Iron Chef Culinary Academy

The ghettoest alliance on Ywain!

Iron Chef Culinary Academy was originally intended to be an "alt guild" for Iron Chef Hibernia. In the tradition of other Hib MLF alt guilds like R2Z, Alpha2, and StormBots, its intent was to help ICH maintain an uber "Against the Odds" score on the Herald. It actually worked for several weeks in the summer of 2003, and ICH shared the "Against the Odds" page with some well known gank guilds. Sadly, most members of ICH left the game, and ICCA (and ICH for that matter) became a guild comprised solely of Eobard's alts.
